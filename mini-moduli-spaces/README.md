## Key Definitions

- `ModuliProblem`: Classifies objects up to isomorphism over varying bases
- `FineModuliSpace`: Represents the moduli functor (has universal family)
- `CoarseModuliSpace`: Corepresents the moduli functor
- `GITQuotient`: Geometric Invariant Theory quotient construction
- `StabilityCondition`: Hilbert-Mumford numerical stability criterion
- `DeformationFunctor`: Formal deformation theory (Schlessinger criteria)
- `AlgebraicStack`: Category fibered in groupoids with representable diagonal
- `DerivedModuliSpace`: Derived enhancement with perfect obstruction theory
- `VirtualFundamentalClass`: Virtual class correcting for excess obstructions

## Key Theorems

- **Grothendieck Hilbert Scheme Existence**: Hilb^P(X) is representable projective
- **Deligne-Mumford**: M_g is a smooth irreducible DM stack of dim 3g-3; Mbar_g is proper
- **Narasimhan-Seshadri**: Moduli of stable bundles on curves exists
- **Stable Reduction**: Limits of smooth curves are stable curves
- **Witten-Kontsevich**: Intersection numbers on Mbar_{g,n} satisfy KdV hierarchy
- **Mumford Tautological Generation**: Chow ring of M_g generated by kappa classes
- **Faber Conjectures**: Tautological ring of M_g is Gorenstein with socle in degree g-2
- **Keel-Mori**: Coarse moduli spaces exist for flat groupoids with finite diagonal
- **Boundedness**: Polarized varieties with fixed Hilbert polynomial form bounded family
- **Unirationality Transition**: M_g unirational for g <= 14, general type for g >= 24
- **Kodaira Dimension**: kappa(M_g) unknown for g=17-21 (major open problem)
